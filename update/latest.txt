v1.3 final (dev branch)

RELEASE NOTES:

Adds iOS 10.3.x downgrade support (WITH MOST SEP FUNCTIONS INTACT) for A8 (excluding A8X) devices

Adds make-custom-ipsw-2 for A8(X) iPad restores using idevicerestore.

Adds macOS support for Apple Silicon Macs and Intel Macs.
Adds iOS 10.3.x downgrade support for A8 (non-A8X) devices with most SEP functions intact.
Adds iOS 11.3–12.4.1 tethered downgrade support for A8(X) iPads.

Adds jailbreak option for seprmvr64 IPSW creation:

Use --jailbreak at the end of the command to create a custom 7.x IPSW with jailbreak already installed.

Brings back --fix-ios8.

Adds iOS 7 tethered downgrade support for iPhone6,2 (Global).
Note: iPhone6,2 supports only iOS 7.0–7.1.2 at the time for seprmvr64-ipsw.

Adds iOS 8.4.1 tethered downgrade support for iPhone 6 and 6 Plus (this uses seprmvr64, so you do lose SEP functions).

Adds iOS 16.6.x downgrade support for A11 devices.
iOS 16.0–16.5.1 is not supported due to Cryptex incompatibility.

Updates localboot for iOS 14+ tethered downgrades.
Fixes A11 deep sleep issues.

Fixes camera and flashlight on iPhone X and iPhone 8 tethered downgrades.
Fixes AVE2 on iOS 15.x tethered restores.
